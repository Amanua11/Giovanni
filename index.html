Our Services

Comprehensive support for your educational journey abroad

Scholarship Guidance

Expert assistance in finding and applying for fully funded Bachelor's, Master's, and PhD scholarships across Europe, Canada, and beyond. We help you identify the best opportunities matching your profile.

Visa Support

Comprehensive visa application support including study visas, work visas, and skilled worker programs for Canada and European countries. We guide you through every step of the process.

University Applications

Step-by-step guidance through university applications, from selecting the right program to submitting compelling applications. We help you present your best self to admissions committees.

Language Preparation

IELTS preparation and language courses to meet university requirements and excel in your studies abroad. Access our Lekora Language Academy for professional instruction.

Free Consultations

Free guidance sessions with live Q&A to help you understand scholarship opportunities, work visas, and study abroad options. Get your questions answered by experts.

Pre-Departure Support

Complete pre-departure briefing including accommodation assistance, cultural orientation, and travel support. We prepare you for a smooth transition to your new country.
