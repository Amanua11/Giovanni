About Giovanni4et

Voice of the Habesha Diaspora - Culture, Migration, Opportunity

Building Dreams, One Student at a Time

Giovanni4et is a leading educational consultancy dedicated to helping Ethiopian and Habesha students navigate the complex journey of studying abroad. We provide comprehensive guidance, from scholarship applications to visa processing, ensuring our students have the best chance of success.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between Ethiopian talent and global opportunities. We believe education is the key to unlocking potential and creating positive change in our communities.

With expertise in European and North American education systems, we offer personalized support tailored to each student's unique goals and circumstances.
